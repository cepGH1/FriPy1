Alkynes CnH2n-2

Molecules with one or more triple covalent,carbon carbon, bonds.
Simple alkynes have the general formula CnH2n-2. Where n is the number of carbon atoms in the molecule.
These are very reactive molecules. The combustion reaction of ethyne is used to provide the heat energy needed to weld metals together.
